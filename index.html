About Me

Tonya Lucas currently works as a Recruitment Coordinator for the Allegis Global Solutions where she’s worked to improve processes, build better systems, and help her team grow. Before joining AGS in November of 2015, Tonya worked as an HR Specialist–Recruiter, serving as the entire recruiting department for a credit union. There, she helped maneuver an equal-partnership merger, the first of its kind in credit unions. She used the opportunity to review and renovate practices while staffing a new and improved organization. Tonya has a Bachelor of Science in Psychology with a concentration in Human Services and a Bachelor of Arts in Dance Studies with a minor in Sociology, all completed at Appalachian State University. She uses her background in psychology to help her understand the world and her background in dance to see the creative perspective. Tonya is currently in her final semester pursuing a Master of Arts in Industrial Organizational Psychology at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, a direction she found through great mentorship and a well-timed introduction to engagement surveys. Tonya believes her greatest strengths lie in the world of talent development, where she can bring together her love of data with her passion for helping others live fulfilled lives. She hopes to help shape the ever-changing and growing world of talent.
