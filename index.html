About Me

I currently work as a Recruitment Coordinator for the Allegis Global Solutions where I’ve worked to improve processes, build better systems, and help my team grow. Before joining AGS in November of 2015, I worked as an HR Specialist–Recruiter, serving as the entire recruiting department for a credit union. There, I helped maneuver an equal-partnership merger, the first of its kind in credit unions. I used the opportunity to review and renovate practices while staffing a new and improved organization. I have a Bachelor of Science in Psychology with a concentration in Human Services and a Bachelor of Arts in Dance Studies with a minor in Sociology, all completed at Appalachian State University. I use my background in psychology to help me understand the world and my background in dance to see the creative perspective. I recently completed a Master of Arts in Industrial Organizational Psychology at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, a direction I found through great mentorship and a well-timed introduction to engagement surveys. I believe my greatest strengths lie in the world of talent development, where I can bring together my love of data with my passion for helping others live fulfilled lives. I hope to help shape the ever-changing and growing world of talent. When I’m not at school or at work, I enjoy travel, eating great food, and coming up with clever (and not- so-clever) puns.
